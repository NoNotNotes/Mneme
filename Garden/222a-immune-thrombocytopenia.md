**Idiopathic** or **immune thrombocytopenic purpura** (ITP), the formerly used term because purpura is an infrequent sign at presentation, is now referred to as immune thrombocytopenia. ITP is an **acquired hemorrhagic disorder** that is characterized by thrombocytopenia, absence to severe signs of bleeding, and normal bone marrow with a normal or increased number of immature megakaryocytes and eosinophils
