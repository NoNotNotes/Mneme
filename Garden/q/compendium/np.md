This is a page compiling as many sample questions I could find online for the PNLE (and other standardized examinations like the RENR and NCLEX). Many of these are from pages e.g. **NurseLabs** and from documents uploaded to file-hosting pages such as **Scribd**, **Studocu** and others.

>[!WARNING] Disclaimers
>1. **Mistakes happen**. I spent over a week collecting, formatting, parsing, and re-formatting dozens of documents for this page. Many documents were PDFs, which I converted via third-party transcoders which utilize OCRs. Missing letters, misread characters, mismatched choices or answers, etc. are expected to be present. I will be correcting these in due time.
>2. **I did not make these questions**. Their content and answer are all based on the documents used for this collection. Always stay open to the possibility of the answer key being wrong. If you have strong inkling that an answer is wrong, you can contact me with the specific question and the correction. Many questions do not have rationalizations, and potentially may not even require them. Cherish the ones that do, because there is no way I'm making rationalizations myself.
>3. **The legality of publishing these questions is questionable**. My use of all related documents are **entirely secondary**, i.e., **I did not obtain them from any original documents**— all documents *were found online*. This is my basis for deciding to publish this despite potential trouble with academic copyright. If any parties would wish for the removal of any section or question, contact me through [GitHub](https://github.com/NoNotNotes). For this same reason, I will not be listing my sources and links to where they were found.
>4. **Questions may be miscategorized**. These questions are broadly categorized between domains they were originally categorized under, but these are not entirely rigid categorizations. I don't mind this myself, so I will not be re-categorizing items anytime soon.
